Fact 1.1.11. The FTE Denominator.

Again, when considering the calculations for FTE, it is important to have a metric by which to compare a course’s enrollment on a full-time basis (hence the term full-time equivalence). A value that normalizes the course membership hours is needed to compare the various courses enrollment contribution to an institution.
As of November 14, 2023 the number that is used to normalize a course’s enrollment contribution is 512. To get this value, the NCCCS considers the average full-time student to have 16 contact hours of courses, multiplied by 16 weeks in a semester, multiplied by 2 semesters in an academic year.
