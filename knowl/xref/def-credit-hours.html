Definition 1.1.3. Credit Hours.

The number of contact hours of a course is different from the credit hours. The number of credit hours is calculated based on a weighted average of the four designations for meeting times (i.e., class, lab, clinical, and work). The weights for the meeting time designations are 1, 0.5, \(\frac{1}{6}\) and 0.10 respectively.
